How to achieve real victory

If we take life as a game, how do we achieve real victory?
The answer is very simple: just do it and work hard. Spend
all your time on your wokr, and you will achieve real victory.
Nothing else matters.

Many people say, "I'm so tired, I can't do that," and so on.
There is only one answer: you don't really want to win. Think
deeper - the thing isn't truly your business. If you really
care about something, you go all in - not just when you feel
like it.
