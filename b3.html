Never sell yourself out

Why do I need a job? Why would I rather work a miserable job for 3k or 4k a month instead of truly improving myself?
You say you need cash flow to survive. But do you really need to waste your time and energy on
a job you hate just to earn a tiny income? You are lying to yourself, and no one can save you from that.
If you have no passion for what you do, you will never become great at it. Your are only wasting
time. Ask yourself honestly: Do you truly love this thing? Or are you only doing it because of pressure,
fear, or other practical reasons? Life is limited. It it not worth wasting your time on things that do
not deserve your heart. Spend your time on things you truly love and believe are valuable.
Enjoy your life.
