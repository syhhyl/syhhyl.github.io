Time is everything

As my first blog post, I want to talk about time.

In my option, time is the most valuable thing I have. There is nothing that can
be exchanged for it on equal terms. In fact, we could say that almost everything
else is obtained through time.

The necessities of life - what you eat, what you wear, what you use - are all
bought with money. And money, in turn, is earned by spending your time: by
working for others in pay, or by running your own business, which also consumes
your time.

In this sense, time can be said to be the most valuable thing, and at the same time
the least valuable. You can choose to do nothing every day, and then you will
have plenty of time—but in that case, time is worth nothing to you.
